Buy together, Better together.

Meet the mobile app that allows you to shop effectively with community members by creating shopping events, splitting bills, and tracking group balances.

Our industry-leading features

  • Shopping Groups

    Offer a unique shopping experience to your community members by creating a shopping event, discussing event details, managing members within the group, and keeping track of your contacts and group activity history.

  • Receipt Scanner

    Processing and converting receipt details into payments by mapping them with a pre-planned shopping list. Accurate outcome and time saving are guaranteed.

  • Split the bill

    Forget about long calculations! This feature allows to split the bill easily and precisely in equal portions or based on the cost of items purchased by each group member.

  • Group Balance

    View group balance at your convenience, efficiently manage your purchases and never miss a payment.

Splink in a few steps

Create or join a shopping event

Create a shopping event, provide the necessary details, and a preliminary shopping list! Or check ongoing events in your neighbourhood and ask to join a group that meets your needs.

Split the bill

Enjoy shopping with neighbours and be surprised by the easy, fast process of in-app receipt recognition as well as the flexibility to choose a method of bill splitting: equally or by members.

Check group balance

Track the shopping group balance presented transparently and accurately. Be notified about payment updates and send or receive reminders to avoid payment delays.
